# Resetting or Changing Your ChatGPT Password

This guide covers password reset and password update steps for ChatGPT accounts.

## Reset your password
If you cannot access your account, start from the ChatGPT login flow.

1. Log out first, or use a private/incognito browser window.
2. Open the ChatGPT home page.
3. Click **Log in**.
4. Enter your email address or phone number.
5. On the password screen, choose **Forgot password?**
6. Follow the instructions sent in the password reset email.

## Change or add a password from settings
If you can already sign in:
1. Open ChatGPT.
2. Go to **Settings > Account**.
3. Add or update your password.

Once a password is set, the same settings page can also be used for account email changes.

## Account scope
Changing the password affects the full OpenAI account, not only ChatGPT. The updated password is also used for other services tied to the same OpenAI identity.

## If you do not receive the reset email
Check the following before opening a support case:
- spam or junk folder
- whether you are checking the same inbox used during signup
- whether the entered email address contains a typo

## Social-login exception
If the account was created only with Google, Microsoft, or Apple login, there may be no password to reset. In that case, sign in through the original provider. Password recovery for those providers must be handled on their own websites.

## Still locked out
If none of the steps work and you still cannot access the account, contact OpenAI Support.
