# Billing Help for Failed ChatGPT Plus or Pro Renewals

This document explains common reasons a subscription renewal payment may fail and what the customer should try next.

## Common causes
A renewal can fail because of:
- card or payment-method problems
- bank-side security checks
- unsupported country or region combinations
- incorrect billing details
- expired card or insufficient funds

## First troubleshooting steps
### 1. Clear browser cache and cookies
Sometimes stored browser data can interfere with payment or checkout behavior. Retry the transaction after clearing cache and cookies.

### 2. Contact your bank
If the failure continues, ask the bank whether:
- the card was blocked
- the payment was flagged for security reasons
- the account has enough available funds

### 3. Verify payment information
Check:
- card number
- expiration date
- billing address
- any other payment details required during renewal

### 4. Confirm country or region support
The payment may fail if either the customer or the card issuer is outside supported regions. Confirm that both are eligible.

## When support is needed
If the payment still fails after the steps above, the customer should contact support through the Help Center chat widget.

## Suggested support summary
When opening a support case, provide:
- plan type
- date of failed renewal
- last four digits of the card if appropriate
- screenshots or exact error message
- country/region details if relevant
