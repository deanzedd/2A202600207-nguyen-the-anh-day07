# How to Change Your Email Address

This guide explains how customers can update the email address connected to their OpenAI account.

## Who can change their email
You can change your email if your account supports email management in ChatGPT on the web. This includes standard email/password accounts and social-login accounts after a password has been added.

Email changes are not supported for:
- phone-number-only accounts
- Enterprise SSO accounts
- some personal accounts using enterprise-verified domains

Your ChatGPT and API Platform account use the same identity. If you change the email in ChatGPT, the sign-in email changes for both.

## Steps on web
1. Sign in to ChatGPT on the web.
2. Open **Settings**.
3. Select **Account**.
4. Click your current email address.
5. Enter the new email and complete verification.

After the change, you will be signed out and must sign in again with the new email.

## Social login users
If you originally signed up with Google, Apple, or Microsoft:
1. Sign in with your social login.
2. Go to **Settings > Account**.
3. Add a password to the account.
4. After that, update the email from the same page.

## Important limitations
- You cannot change your email from the iOS or Android app.
- There are limits on how often the email can be changed.
- You cannot switch to an email that already belongs to another OpenAI account.
- You cannot reuse a previous email to create a new account.
- Chat history and workspaces cannot be transferred to a different account.

## If you cannot change the email
If the option is unavailable, the fallback is usually to create a new account. If you need access to the same API organization, add the new account to that organization separately.
