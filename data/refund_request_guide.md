# How to Request a Refund for a ChatGPT Subscription

This guide explains how refund requests work for ChatGPT subscriptions and how the process differs by purchase channel.

## General rule
The refund process depends on where the subscription was purchased. To speed up verification, the request should be submitted while signed in to the same OpenAI account that was charged, or from the email address tied to that charge.

## If the subscription was purchased on the web
1. Log in to ChatGPT with the charged account.
2. Open the Help Center.
3. Use the chat widget in the bottom-right corner.
4. Submit a refund request there.

The system may show a quick eligibility summary before a human agent continues the process.

## If the chat widget cannot find the subscription
If the widget says login is required or cannot locate the subscription:
- sign in to help.openai.com with the correct charged account
- reopen the widget
- submit the request again

## Processing timelines
- Web purchases that are approved are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days.
- Google Play purchases that are approved are typically processed within 10 business days.
- Accidental purchases are generally eligible if reported within 14 days of the charge.

## Apple subscriptions
If the subscription was purchased through Apple, refunds and cancellations must be handled directly with Apple.

## Regional note
Customers in the EU, UK, and Turkey may be eligible for a prorated refund if they cancel within 14 days of purchase.

## After refund processing
A receipt is sent to the email associated with the account. If it is missing, check the spam folder.
